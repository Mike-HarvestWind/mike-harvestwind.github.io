Friends,

I started Harvest Wind to invest in and operate high quality small businesses in industries that steward the environment (both the organic and built worlds) or human health. We prioritize long-term business-building over short-term financial returns, humility, a sense of humor, thematic focus, and operational collaboration.

We are a holding company which intentionally affords us the flexibility to invest for the long-term (thinking in decades rather than quarters) unlike traditional private equity funds, while our dedication to deploying capital efficiently and patiently allows us to target services or markets traditionally unfit / misfit for venture capitalists.

We focus on identifying underserved but highly sustainable niches through deep industry research. We invest to operate alongside our partners, prioritizing hands-on collaboration with founders and management teams (after all, the hard work of building is the most fun).

Above all, we aim to approach the world of investing and operating without ego and aim to always be earnest, curious, and humble in whatever we do.
