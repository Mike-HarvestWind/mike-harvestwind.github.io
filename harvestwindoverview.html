Dear friends,

I started Harvest Wind to invest in small and mid-sized businesses that make the world a better place through agriculture, food, or the environment. We prioritize long-term business-building over short-term financial returns, humility, a sense of humor, thematic focus, and operational collaboration.

Our holding company structure affords us the flexibility to invest for the long-term (thinking in decades rather than quarters) unlike traditional private equity funds while our dedication to deploying capital efficiently and patiently allows us to target services or markets traditionally unfit for venture capitalists.

We focus on identifying underserved but highly sustainable niches through deep industry research. We invest to operate alongside our partners, prioritizing hands-on collaboration with founders and management teams (after all, the hard work of building is the most fun).

Above all, we aim to approach the world of investing with an egoless attitude and aim to always be earnest, curious, and humble in whatever we do.

Sincerely,

Founder & CEO
