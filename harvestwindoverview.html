Dear friends,

I started Harvest Wind to invest in small and mid-sized businesses serving fragmented and/or complex value chains across agriculture, food, and the environment. We prioritize long-term partnership over short-term investing, humility, thematic focus, and operational collaboration.

Our structure affords us the flexibility to invest for the long-term (thinking in decades rather than years) unlike traditional private equity firms, while our dedication to deploying capital efficiently and patiently allows us to target solutions or markets traditionally unfit for venture capital.

We focus on identifying underserved niches within the agrifood and environmental services space via deep industry research. We invest to operate alongside our partners, prioritizing hands-on collaboration with founders and management teams (after all, the hard work of building is the most fun).

Above all, we aim to approach the world of investing and operating with an egoless attitude and aim to always be earnest, curious, and humble in whatever we do.
